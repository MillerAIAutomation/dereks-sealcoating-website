Our Story

WE STRIVE FOR
THE BEST

I started this company after my father — with over 3 decades of experience — taught me the tricks of the trade. Over time we've evolved our system as well as our materials. We started with small brushes and Home Depot buckets and turned our profits into commercial-grade materials and equipment for a better product for our clients.

Every single day we strive to perfect our craft because there is always room to improve. We guarantee all of our work and will answer any questions or concerns once we have finished the job. We always prioritize the customer and want to give the best result to match your budget.

We also don't splash any sealer on your beautiful concrete or home — because we wouldn't want sealer on ours. We guarantee all of our work for a minimum of 1 year; however, our sealer will last 2–4 years depending on the conditions of the driveway.

How We Work

OUR STANDARDS

We Prep Properly

Every crack is cleaned and filled. Edges are taped. The surface is blown clear of debris. We don't skip steps — because sealcoating over unprepared pavement is a waste of everyone's time and money.

We Use Real Product

We use commercial-grade sealer designed for airports and parking lots — not watered-down commodity product from a hardware store. We add sand for durability and traction, applied at full strength for maximum protection.

We Respect Your Property

Sealer stays on the driveway. We edge carefully, protect your lawn and garage floor, and clean up completely when we're done. You shouldn't have to worry about overspray — and with us, you won't.
