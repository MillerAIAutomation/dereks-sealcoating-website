Services

Derek's Sealcoating provides the following services:

  • Residential and commercial driveway and parking lot sealcoating
  • Crack sealing and crack filling
  • Pothole and wide crack repair
  • Line striping and sport court marking

All services are subject to availability, weather conditions, and the physical condition of your pavement. We reserve the right to decline or modify a project if site conditions make safe or effective work impossible.

Estimates

All estimates provided by Derek's Sealcoating — whether by phone, email, or on-site visit — are free and carry no obligation. Estimates are based on the visible condition of the pavement at the time of assessment. Final pricing may be adjusted if additional damage or conditions are discovered upon arrival that were not apparent during the initial estimate.

Estimates are valid for 30 days from the date provided.

Scheduling and Weather

Sealcoating requires dry conditions and temperatures above 50°F to cure properly. We reserve the right to reschedule any job due to rain, cold weather, or other conditions that would compromise the quality of the finished product. We will contact you as soon as possible if a reschedule is necessary. No cancellation fees apply for weather-related rescheduling.

Customer Responsibilities

To allow us to complete your job properly, you agree to:

  • Remove all vehicles, personal property, and obstructions from the work area before our crew arrives
  • Ensure the driveway or lot is accessible on the scheduled date
  • Keep pets and children off the work area during and after application until the sealer has cured (24–48 hours for foot traffic, 48–72 hours for vehicles)
  • Notify us of any underground utilities, irrigation heads, or drainage concerns prior to work beginning

Derek's Sealcoating is not responsible for damage resulting from failure to follow post-application care instructions.
