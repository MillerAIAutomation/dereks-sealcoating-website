PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Derek's Sealcoating ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information you provide when visiting our website or requesting our services. By using our site or submitting a form, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you fill out our estimate request form or contact us directly. This may include:

  • First and last name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Property address or service location
  • Description of your project

We do not collect payment information through our website. We also collect basic usage data automatically (pages visited, browser type, approximate location) through standard web server logs.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your estimate request or inquiry
  • Schedule and complete sealcoating services
  • Send you confirmations, reminders, or follow-up communications related to your project
  • Send you SMS text messages and emails as described below

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

SMS Text Message Communications

By submitting our contact form or providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Derek's Sealcoating. These messages may include:

  • Confirmation that your estimate request was received
  • Appointment reminders and scheduling updates
  • Follow-up messages regarding your project

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. To opt out of SMS messages at any time, reply STOP to any message we send. For help, reply HELP or contact us at 610-635-8830.

We respect your time and will not send unsolicited promotional text blasts. Communications will be directly related to your inquiry or service.

Email Communications

By submitting our contact form, you may receive email confirmation and follow-up from Derek's Sealcoating. You can opt out of future emails at any time by replying "unsubscribe" or contacting us directly at dereksealcoating@gmail.com.

Third-Party Services

Our website and operations use the following third-party services, which may process your information under their own privacy policies:

  • GoHighLevel — CRM and communication platform used to manage form submissions, send SMS messages, and send emails. See GoHighLevel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Maps — embedded map on our contact page. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — font delivery service. Requests are made to Google servers when you visit our site.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies — primarily technical cookies required for basic site functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or analytics at this time.

Data Retention

We retain your contact information only as long as necessary to complete your service request and for a reasonable period afterward in case of follow-up. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of SMS or email communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at dereksealcoating@gmail.com or call 610-635-8830.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
